About Us

We exist to help corporations, buyers, sellers and make what you want.

Our Mission

eSupplier Portal believes that Buyers and Suppliers should have easy, online access to developing and sustaining relationships. Procurement Registration's mission is to give small and large corporations, organizations, and other entities a path to forge these working relationships and to allow them to continue to grow

A letter from our founder

eSupplier Portal was founded upon the premise that Buyers and Suppliers need an expedient, accurate way to develop relationships with one another. Business relationships are not unlike personal relationships: they can be difficult and fragile if not handled with care. Procurement Registration takes the guesswork out of building these new relationships so that both Buyers and Suppliers can concentrate on their own areas of expertise and can leave the business of building new business relationships to us.

eSupplier Portal is the only Business Intelligence Application and Platform for Suppliers and Buyers to utilize, discover and do business with each other.

eSupplier Portal simplifies the Corporate Buying Portal Registration Process for Suppliers.

With one single Supplier Registration on eSupplier Portal you and your company is now in our system and can be found by Corporate Buyers. With one single Supplier Registration, Suppliers can register on hundreds of Corporate Buying Portals (via ProReg Assisted Submission). You can update, change your details, and you can choose exactly which Corporate Buyers you want to target at any time!
